# Making a Difference in the Community: Chiropractic Care & Charity Talk for Ti-Ratana Welfare

To us, this charity event was not just another event.

> To us, this charity event was not just another event. It was a chance for Persistence to give back to the community and help those in need. As a chiropractic care provider, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality spine care, regardless of their financial status.

> This event allowed us to reach out to the underprivileged and provide them with the care they deserve. It was truly a humbling experience, and we are honoured to have had the opportunity to contribute to our community in a meaningful way.

Persistence Chiropractic Care, a Kuala Lumpur based chiropractic and physiotherapy clinic recently collaborated with Managedcare and Lunox for a charity event at Ti-Ratana Welfare Society that aimed to make a difference in the community.

The primary purpose of this event was to educate people about the seriousness of spine health and the significance of chiropractic care in maintaining it. Through this event, the organisers aimed to create awareness about the daily wear and tear that our spines endure and how chiropractic care can help minimise its effects.

The reason Ti-Ratana Welfare was chosen as the venue for the charity was because the residents there are often overlooked and may not have the means to receive proper medical attention, being an orphanage and old folks home.

By bringing the event to Ti-Ratana Welfare, Persistence were able to reach out to these underserved communities to improve the health and well-being of all individuals, regardless of their socio-economic background.

With this mission in mind, they organised a series of events that took place throughout the month of March:

## 1. Chiropractic Talk at Ti-Ratana Welfare

The event kicked off with a chiropractic and health talk at Ti-Ratana Welfare on the 1st March 2023. 

The talk covered various topics, including:

1. Benefits of chiropractic care and spinal health tips presented by D.C. Valerie and D.C. Derek from Persistence Chiropractic Care.

2. Education on importance of sleep hygiene by the D.C. Giselle and Dr. Daniel from Lunox team.

3. Simple at-home exercises for posture were demonstrated by Physiotherapists experts from Managedcare.

## 2. Spinal Screening

The talk was then followed by a free Spinal Screening for all residents and staffs of Ti-Ratana Welfare by our chiropractors. The screening was conducted to identify any spinal issues that the residents might have and provide them with guidance on how to address these issues.

## 3. Spinal X-Ray at Sunway Medical Velocity

On 11th March, all the residents of the Ti-Ratana Welfare headed to Sunway Velocity Medical for a spinal X-Ray fully sponsored by Sunway Medical. The X-Ray provided a more in-depth look of each resident’s spine, to allow the chiropractors to identify any underlying issues that may not have been visible during the screening.

## 4. Spinal Adjustment Treatments at Persistence Chiropractic Care

The final event of the month was held on 26th March 2023, where all the patients were invited to Persistence clinic to receive their first chiropractic treatment. During the treatment sessions, the chiropractors carefully examined the patients' spinal health and provided personalized treatment plans throughout the year .

They utilized advanced diagnostic tools, including X-rays, to gain a deeper understanding of the patients' conditions and identify any underlying issues that may be contributing to their pain or discomfort.

Based on the X-ray results, the chiropractors were able to explain to the patients the root cause of their spinal issues. They also provided valuable advice on how to maintain good spinal health, such as regular exercise and proper posture.

--

Overall, the event was a tremendous success. It provided an opportunity for Persistence Chiropractic Care to raise awareness about the importance of spinal health and how chiropractic care can help individuals lead a healthy life.

The collaboration with Managedcare and Lunox helped make the event possible, and the generous sponsorship from Sunway Velocity Medical. The event was an excellent example of how community collaboration can make a difference in people's lives, and Persistence Chiropractic Care hopes to continue to provide more charity events in the future to promote spinal health and help individuals lead a healthy life.
