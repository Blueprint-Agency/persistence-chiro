Page Architecture — Two Pillars
Service Pillar

"What we do"

Treatment & technique pages. Each targets commercial-intent keywords and converts visitors into bookings.

Problem Pillar

"What you feel"

Condition & symptom pages. Captures patients searching for their pain, then links them to the right service page.

Every blog post links up to its parent pillar page → builds topical authority Google rewards. Service ↔ Problem pages cross-link where relevant. Blog comparison posts (e.g. tit tar vs chiropractic) capture adjacent audiences and funnel them to service pages.
